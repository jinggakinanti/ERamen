About Us

ERamen is one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. It was established in Japan from 2000 and brought to Indonesia since 2010. All ERamen menu made by organic flour and turn into delicious ramen noodle. We can find various types of ramen.

Vision: To be the premier destination for ramen lovers, known for serving the most authentic and delicious ramen dishes that capture the essence of Japanese culinary tradition.

Mission: To consistently serve the highest quality ramen, made with carefully selected ingredients and crafted with precision.
